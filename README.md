# 利基探测器 / Niche Finder

**项目定位：一个 AI 驱动的创业机会发现引擎，专为独立开发者和初创团队设计，致力于发掘具有商业潜力的细分市场（Niche Market）。**

> AI 驱动的创业机会发现引擎。从市场数据出发，帮独立创业者找到「小而美、获客快、盈利快」的真实利基机会。

## What is Niche Finder?
**Niche Finder** is an AI-powered opportunity discovery engine. It helps independent developers, solopreneurs, and startup teams find profitable, uncrowded niche markets. By inputting your skills, budget, and interests, Niche Finder leverages AI agents to conduct market research, competitor analysis, and business viability assessments.

## Why this project exists
Most startup ideas fail because they target overly competitive "red oceans" or solve non-existent problems. Niche Finder exists to:
- Take the guesswork out of finding a business idea.
- Provide data-driven, objective feedback on your ideas before you write a single line of code.
- Force ideas through a "Devil's Advocate" mechanism to highlight risks and weaknesses early.

## Core features
- **4 Discovery Modes**: 
  - **Blue Ocean**: Discover entirely new niches.
  - **Red Niche**: Find specific, unserved segments within existing crowded markets.
  - **Cross Domain**: Apply successful models from industry A to industry B.
  - **Deep Analysis**: Get a comprehensive teardown of a specific idea.
- **6-Dimension Evaluation Model**: Evaluates ideas on market demand, competition, monetization, technical feasibility, timing, and team fit.
- **Devil's Advocate Mechanism**: An agent specifically designed to poke holes in the idea to prevent "echo chamber" thinking.
- **Multi-Agent Collaboration**: Utilizes 6 distinct AI agents (Product, Research, Competitor Analysis, User Research, Business Design, and Devil's Advocate) working together.

## Example use cases
- **The Solo Developer**: Has strong technical skills but isn't sure what to build. Uses the *Blue Ocean* mode to find a B2B SaaS tool with low competition.
- **The Domain Expert**: Wants to digitize a process in their specific industry. Uses the *Deep Analysis* mode to validate the idea and uncover potential pitfalls.
- **The Serial Maker**: Looking to pivot a struggling product. Uses the *Cross Domain* mode to find new applications for their existing technology.
